About us
We're Synergazia, a company with the simple goal of simpliying online group collaboration. We believe it should be easier to remotely work together and communicate with teammates online whether it is for school, work or even just a project with friends. We plan to keep innovating on our ideas and find many new ways of making online collaboration as efficient as it is in person.
How do we plan to do this?
Our inital goal to do this was to create a product called Synergazia. This will be an online platform free platform available to any user who signs up to our website. It will provide users with a way to create and join other users projects. Along with different ways to communicate and collaborate online aswell. Our planned release is on the timeline so if you are interested please contact us at Synergazia@gmail.com

Synergazia A Place Where You Can...
Invite, collaborate, succeed
Make project management easier with personalised project rooms tailored to your needs. Synergazia servers can be organized into different group channels where you can collaborate, share, and just talk to one another.
Seemless file, image and audio sharing
We provide live communication with people inside your project. We support most major file types for instant file sharing and accessibility for all team members. Synergazia also supports multi-media messaging allowing users to send images and voice messages to one another to aid in online collaboration.
Tools for administrators to monitor, customize, and optimize
Get any project up and running with administrave tools to edit channels inside your project allowing you to sepate work evenly amongst big teams. Give members moderation powers to keep your projects managed even when you're not there.
Unleash your creativity
Synergazia provides a dynamic whiteboard space for users to draw, brainstorm, and collaborate on. Upload designs and rapidly pitch ideas together or create collaborative art online from scratch. Our custom whiteboards create a space in your projects for you team to come together and really collaborate with one another.
